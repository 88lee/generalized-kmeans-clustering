    Bregman Divergence defines a convex function F and its gradient.

    For convenience, we also provide methods for evaluating F and its gradient when points are provided using homogeneous coordinates. Evaluating F and gradient of F on homogeneous coordinates is often more efficient that mapping the homogeneous coordinates to inhomogeneous coordinates and then evaluating F and gradient F.
